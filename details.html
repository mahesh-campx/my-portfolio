About Me

I am a Software Developer Trainee at CampX Digital Campus Ecosystem. working on real-world products used by colleges and universities. I build responsive front-end applications using React and develop scalable backend services with Node.js / NestJS. I work with REST and GraphQL APIs, integrating them into our micro-service-based architecture. I also customize Frappe / ERPNext modules to automate workflows for academic institutions. My development approach focuses on clean code, readability, and maintainability. I enjoy translating product requirements into functional UI and API implementations. My background in networking (Cisco/Juniper, routing/switching) helps me understand systems end-to-end. I collaborate closely with designers, QA, and product teams to ship features smoothly. Learning and improving every day is something I strongly believe in. My goal is to grow into a full-stack product engineer and build software that solves real problems.
